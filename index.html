Preparing your garments for collecting

In order to ensure collection of your garments by our sales peoples, we encourage our clients to follow these tips

The timing given upon booking are only approximate and depend on a range of issues beyond our control, thus we kindly ask you to be patient. Particularlly in summer and due to the workload, our sales people work alone and may not always be able to drive and call client before arriving.
